## Personal data and peer identity

This e-portfolio contains primary evidence of my work on the Machine Learning module. Some evidence may include emails, names and other PII, GDPR and UK GDPR governed data, such as email exchanges from the Team Project.

To respect privacy and align with the different regulations, I have taken these measures:

- The names and email addresses of my peer group members have been blacked out on every published evidence document. My own name and email address remain visible because this is my e-portfolio.
- Tutor identity (Elizabeth Oladapo, Module Tutor) is preserved where her communications are referenced, because her professional role and her published University identity make this appropriate.
- No claim made in this e-portfolio depends on identifying any individual peer. Claims about peer contribution are framed by role or workstream.

If anything on this site appears to identify a peer or anyone else by mistake, please [open a GitHub issue](https://github.com/protode908/eportfolio-uoe/issues) and I will redact it immediately.

## Copyright and fair-dealing

Some pieces of evidence on this e-portfolio reproduce third-party content as inputs or stimuli for a transformative analysis. Specifically, the Unit 10 CNN Explainer walkthrough reproduces:

- Three third-party photographs used as model inputs: Vonore Middle School (2025) for the US yellow school bus, TN (2024) for the Argentinian orange school bus, and Williamson (2024) via Alamy for the NYC yellow taxi. The fourth Unit 10 input (the selfie) is the author's own photograph.
- Screenshots of the Wang et al. (2020) CNN Explainer interactive visualisation.

These third-party materials are reproduced under UK fair-dealing for criticism, review and quotation (Copyright, Designs and Patents Act 1988, s.30) as inputs to a transformative object-recognition experiment. Each item is attributed inline on the corresponding card and in the consolidated [References](/eportfolio-uoe/references/).

If a rightsholder believes attribution is incorrect or wishes the material removed, please [open a GitHub issue](https://github.com/protode908/eportfolio-uoe/issues) and the image will be removed or further attributed.
